Understanding Different Types of Waste

Learn about the various types of waste and how proper management can contribute to a cleaner environment:

1. Organic Waste

Includes biodegradable materials such as food scraps, yard waste, and other natural substances. Proper disposal and composting can help reduce organic waste.

2. Recyclable Waste

Consists of materials that can be recycled, such as paper, cardboard, plastic, glass, and certain metals. Recycling helps conserve resources and reduce landfill waste.

3. Hazardous Waste

Includes materials that pose a threat to the environment and human health. Examples include batteries, electronic waste (e-waste), and certain chemicals. Proper disposal is crucial for safety.

4. E-Waste (Electronic Waste)

Comprises discarded electronic devices, including old computers, mobile phones, and appliances. E-waste recycling is essential to prevent environmental contamination.
